Oussama Hamza (maths)

I am joining the Institute for Advanced Studies in Mathematics (IASM) at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) as an assistant researcher (postdoc).

I completed my PhD thesis at Western University, under the supervision of Christian Maire and Jan Minac, which is available here.
My work mostly deals with pro-p groups filtrations and associated graded Lie algebras. I am also interested in Arithmetic applications and Galois groups in a broad sense.
